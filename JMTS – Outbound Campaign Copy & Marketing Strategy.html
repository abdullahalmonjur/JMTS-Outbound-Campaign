Marketing Strategy & Ideas
7 actionable recommendations
01
LinkedIn Thought Leadership Content
Post 3–4x per week on LinkedIn as Rabiul Nibir, targeting Amazon seller pain points. Topics: "5 mistakes Amazon sellers make when sourcing from China", "Why DDP is better than DAP for FBA sellers", "How we reduced one client's COGS by 22%". Personal posts consistently outperform company pages. Build an audience before the sale.
LinkedInOrganicLong-term
02
Targeted LinkedIn Sales Navigator Outreach
Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to filter Amazon sellers by title (Amazon FBA Seller, E-commerce Director, Private Label Brand Owner), geography (UK, USA, EU, Middle East), and company size. Build a list of 200–300 prospects monthly and run the touch sequence in this document. Personalize the first line of each message with a detail from their profile.
LinkedInPaid ToolHigh ROI
03
Amazon Seller Facebook Groups & Forums
Join active Amazon seller communities on Facebook (e.g., Amazon FBA Ninjas, Proven Amazon Course Community) and Reddit (r/FulfillmentByAmazon). Provide genuine value by answering sourcing and logistics questions. Mention JMTS only when contextually appropriate. Build trust first — this is a slow burn but generates highly qualified inbound leads.
CommunityInboundFree
04
Alibaba Store Optimization & Review Showcase
Your 100% five-star Alibaba record is a massive trust signal — but most prospects won't see it unless you push it. Add the Alibaba review link to every email signature, LinkedIn bio, and outreach message. Create a short video testimonial compilation from existing clients and share it as a LinkedIn post. Social proof converts faster than any sales copy.
Trust-buildingAlibabaSocial Proof
05
YouTube / Short-Form Video Content
Create short (60–90 second) videos showing the behind-the-scenes of JMTS operations — warehouse footage, factory visits, packing and labelling, shipping containers. Amazon sellers trust suppliers they can "see". Post on YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn Video, and TikTok. Titles like "How we prep Amazon FBA inventory in Guangzhou" perform extremely well with this audience.
VideoYouTubeTikTok
06
Email Newsletter for Warm Leads
Build a simple monthly newsletter for anyone who has engaged with you but hasn't converted. Include: China market updates, FBA regulation changes, shipping cost forecasts, and a client success story. Keep it under 300 words. Tools like Mailchimp (free tier) make this easy. Staying top of mind is crucial — most B2B deals close after 6–8 touchpoints.
EmailNurtureLow cost
07
Referral Program for Existing Clients
Your existing Amazon seller clients (70–80% of your base) are your strongest marketing asset. Create a simple referral incentive — e.g., a discount on their next shipment for every qualified referral that converts. Amazon seller communities are tight-knit; word of mouth is powerful. A structured referral program formalizes this and gives clients a reason to recommend you proactively.
ReferralWord-of-mouthRetention
